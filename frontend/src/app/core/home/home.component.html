Textcavator is a tool for exploring corpora (large collections of texts). You can use textcavator to find relevant documents or visualise wider trends in the corpus.

Designed with and for researchers in the humanities and social sciences, Textcavator offers an accessible interface to search a wide variety of corpora, such as newspaper archives, online book reviews, and judicial hearings. Additional corpora are available for members of Utrecht University.

Find out more about Textcavator on our about page, or start searching a corpus below.
